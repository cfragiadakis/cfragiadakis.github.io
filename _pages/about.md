I’m a Data Scientist currently completing my MSc in Information Studies (Data Science track) at the [University of Amsterdam](https://www.uva.nl/). My interests lie in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, Large Language Models, and trustworthy AI systems.

My Master’s thesis focuses on Fairness-Aware Multimodal Transformer Modeling for Real-Time Student Attention Estimation, where I developed deep learning models combining visual and wearable sensor data to investigate both predictive performance and fairness across demographic groups.

Before my MSc, I worked as a Data Scientist in the consulting sector at EY and Grant Thornton, contributing to projects involving Large Language Models, information extraction, data analytics, and end-to-end AI solutions using technologies such as Python, LangChain, Docker, and Microsoft Azure.

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Management Science & Technology from the [Athens University of Economics and Business](https://www.aueb.gr/), with major in Software Engineering and Data Science. My undergraduate thesis explored advancements in Natural Language Processing.

I have also contributed to academic research projects, including studies on Greek tourism through large-scale text analysis, applying sentiment analysis and topic modeling techniques to extract insights from thousands of international and Greek press articles.
